How To Use This App

Step 1:
On the home page click "Create New House".

Step 2:
Fill out all required information and press "Save Changes".

Step 3:
You will now be able to find the "Damaged House" you just created in the "Houses" tab in the dropdown menu.

Step 4:
Once you are in the "Houses" tab you will see a list of differnt house estimates. Find the one you have just created and click open estimate.

Step 5:
Once you are in the "Damaged House" you just clicked on, press "Add Room".

Step 6:
Now that you have added a room fill out the requried feilds. You can add as many rooms as you need to a "Damaged House".
